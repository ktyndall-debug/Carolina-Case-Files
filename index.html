The Investigation Begins

Welcome to Carolina Case Files, where the shadows of the past refuse to stay buried. We delve deep into the most perplexing cold cases and cryptic mysteries that have haunted the Carolinas for decades.

Each episode takes you on a journey through forgotten evidence, overlooked clues, and testimonies that time tried to silence. From unexplained disappearances in the Blue Ridge Mountains to mysterious deaths along the Carolina coast, we explore cases that challenge conventional explanations.

Join us as we piece together fragments of truth, interview those who remember, and shine new light on cases that authorities couldn't solve. Some mysteries have answers waiting to be discovered. Others may forever remain in the shadows.
