The Story That Started It All

What began as a single investigation into The Pender County Rake exploded across the internet overnight. Our YouTube channel went from zero to thousands of subscribers as the chilling footage and eyewitness accounts captivated viewers hungry for the truth.

Now, we're working tirelessly to bring you more unsolved mysteries from across the Carolinas. Each episode requires extensive research, careful filming in remote locations, and meticulous editing to preserve the integrity of these cases.

New episodes are coming soon. Quality investigations take time, but we promise each story will be worth the wait. Subscribe to our channel and turn on notifications so you don't miss when we uncover the next mystery lurking in the shadows of the Carolinas.
